You & Me
2025

Started together.
Learned distance.
Stayed us.

Before August,
every day felt extra special.

Not because we tried.
But because it was with you.

Sitting in a bakery in Manali.
No rush. No noise.
Just us and time slowing down.

Watching Shinchan Movie together.
Laughing at stupid scenes.

Somehow, that meant everything.

Together since: 30.11.2023
Cities apart: Delhi ⇄ Baddi
Video calls: Few, but priceless
Laughs during calls: Too many to hide
Fights in 2025: Yes
Giving up on us: Zero

In August, distance entered.

Not between us.

But between our days.

Video calls where I talked
and you replied on text.

You laughing silently
so your parents wouldn’t hear.

Me trying harder
just to see you smile.

Before August,
my gallery was full.

Random photos.
Random bakchodi.

After August,
it was mostly empty.

December 21.
Two days.
Hectic. Tiring. Perfect.

Even without nights together,
it still felt like home.

Distance taught me this:

Love isn’t access.
Love is effort.

Showing up differently
still counts as showing up.

Thank you for trusting in us.

For staying playful.
For staying kind.

For staying mine.

New year.
Same us.

New memories waiting.

I’d rewind it all.

And still choose you.
